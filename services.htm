  PERSONALIZED MENU
Your food preferences are recorded during an in-home interview with Chef Jenny Lou. Taking into account any special dietary needs, a menu is prepared and submitted to you for approval prior to the scheduled cooking day.

All cooking takes place in your kitchen, with fresh ingredients purchased on the cooking day.

 
  CONVENIENT
On the day of your service, your Personal Chef shops, cooks, and cleans up. You will come home to a clean kitchen filled with wonderful aromas and tonight's dinner.

Chef Jenny Lou brings all of her own cooking utensils and tools, down to her favorite wire whisk.

  
  Appetizing and nutritious meals are packaged in disposable containers for storage in your refrigerator and freezer.

Each container is labeled with instructions for quick and easy re-heating.

 

  
 

[Your customized menu folder includes a record of all ingredients used and also includes the re-heating instructions for your referral without having to open the freezer door. Chef Jenny Lou also keeps a record of your menu choices in order to help plan future service.]

  
  NUTRITIOUS
Your Chef uses the freshest and best ingredients available—vegetables, fruits, meats, & fish. No preservatives. Fresh herbs when possible.

Care is taken to meet special dietary needs. Chef Jenny Lou shops selectively in order to assure that your requirements are met.
